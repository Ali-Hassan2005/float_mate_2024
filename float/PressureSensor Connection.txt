SPI PINS

------------------|-----------|--------------

D13             -->   VSCLK   <-- SCLK (GREY)
D9              -->   MCLK    <-- MCLK (BLUE)
GND             -->   GND     <-- WHITE
5V              -->   VCC     <-- VCC (YELLOW)
