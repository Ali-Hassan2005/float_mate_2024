VSPI PINS ON ESP32

ESP32              | UNIVERSAL | PRESSURE SENSOR
------------------|-----------|--------------
D23             -->   MOSI    <-- DIN  (GREEN)
D19             -->   MISO    <-- DOUT (PURPLE)
D18             -->   VSCLK   <-- SCLK (GREY)
D9 (FROM NANO)  -->   32K_CLK <-- MCLK (BLUE)
GND             -->   GND     <-- WHITE
3.3V            -->   VCC     <-- VCC (YELLOW)
